Privacy Policy – Warranty Buddy Last updated: [02.01.2026] Warranty Buddy respects your privacy. DATA COLLECTION Warranty Buddy does not require account registration and does not collect personal data such as name, email address, or phone number. All warranty data, including product names, prices, dates, and photos, is stored locally on your device only. NOTIFICATIONS The app uses local notifications to remind users about warranty expirations. These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on the device. ADS The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device-related data such as advertising identifiers according to Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy DATA SHARING The app does not share your personal data with the developer or third parties. DATA SECURITY Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY Warranty Buddy is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
